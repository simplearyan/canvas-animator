About Studio

Animator Studio is a high-fidelity research lab dedicated to pushing the boundaries of browser-based motion graphics and interactive design prototypes.

The Mission

Our goal is to bridge the gap between static design and interactive motion. By leveraging the power of modern web technologies like HTML5 Canvas and WebCodecs, we enable designers and developers to create cinema-grade animations directly in the browser.

The Technology

Everything built here is powered by the mediabunny core—a specialized framework optimized for frame-perfect recording and real-time visualization. Whether it's complex mathematical waveforms or artistic whiteboard engines, our tools are built for precision and performance.

Open Research

We believe in open collaboration. The Animator Studio repository serves as a blueprint for high-fidelity components, encouraging the community to experiment, fork, and build upon our research.
